BFF Dog Rescue NJ Inc.

Adopt Don't Shop

BFF Dog Rescue NJ, Inc. is an all-volunteer, no-kill, all-breed dog rescue 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. We have no paid employees and we depend on the financial kindness of animal lovers to continue our work in saving the lives of the dogs we rescue. We host adoption events in Brick, N.J. and the surrounding areas, so check us out on Facebook for an up-to-date list of events. If you are interested in fostering or adopting one of our dogs, please use the button below to download an adoption application and email the completed document to bffdogrescuenj@gmail.com. We will review all applications within two to three days. We require a veterinarian and reference check. Our adoption fee helps to cover our expenses and includes all shots that the dog is old enough to receive, fecal check, spay/neuter, and any other fees required when we rescue. The adoption fee for puppies is $350, adult dogs are $300 and older/senior dogs fees are evaluated on a individual basis.
